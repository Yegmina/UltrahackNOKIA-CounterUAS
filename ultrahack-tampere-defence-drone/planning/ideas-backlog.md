# Ideas Backlog

## Strongest Ideas

- Counter-UAS operator confidence dashboard.
- Multi-sensor evidence fusion with simple explainable scoring.
- Drone detection validation harness for degraded indoor conditions.
- Signal-health monitor for drone-ground links under jamming-like conditions.
- False-positive reducer for camera/audio/RF detections.

## Fast Prototype Ideas

- OpenCV motion detector plus tracker and confidence timeline.
- Replay-driven dashboard using prepared drone footage.
- Synthetic packet-loss/latency simulator for telemetry health.
- Audio anomaly detector from microphone input.
- "Last known sector" arena map with uncertainty radius.

## Differentiators

- Honest uncertainty instead of binary alarms.
- Repeatable test metrics.
- Works live or offline.
- Fits multiple sensors.
- Designed around operator decision support.

## Avoid

- Autonomous engagement.
- Physical interception mechanisms.
- Guidance for evading counter-UAS systems.
- Any demo that depends on unsafe flight behavior.
- A pitch that claims military readiness from a weekend prototype.
