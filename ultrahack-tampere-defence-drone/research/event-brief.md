# Event Brief

Updated: 7 June 2026

## What The Event Is

Drone Innovation Hackathon 2 is a follow-up drone innovation hackathon at Nokia Arena in Tampere. Business Tampere describes it as a June 2026 event where teams return to build, test, and refine drone solutions with experts in a real indoor arena setting. The event is in English.

## Key Facts

- Name: Drone Innovation Hackathon 2
- Dates: 9 June 2026, 16:00 to 11 June 2026, 15:30
- Venue: Nokia Arena
- Address: Kansikatu 3, 33100 Tampere
- Language: English
- Registration deadline reported by partner posts: 24 May 2026
- Prize pool reported by UltraHack/partner posts for Hackathon 2: EUR 10,000
- Reported follow-on opportunities: 17Tech Defence Accelerator funding opportunity, Digital Defence Ecosystem membership, and selected-team accommodation packages.

## Previous January Challenge Context

The January Drone Innovation at 5G Counter-Drone Arena event had these listed challenges:

- The Invisible Intruder: low-visibility/evasive drone concepts tested against advanced counter-drone systems.
- 5G-Driven Drone Innovation: BVLOS-style obstacle-course flight using 4G/5G for video and control.
- Low Signature Drone Design: quiet approach toward an Elisa Defence Node base station without being detected.

These are not necessarily the exact June challenge statements, but they reveal the organizers' priorities: live arena testing, defence-relevant robustness, 5G connectivity, low-signature behavior, detection, tracking, and counter-drone validation.

## Practical Implications

- Treat the event as validation-oriented, not only ideation-oriented.
- Prepare something that can be shown under real constraints inside Nokia Arena.
- Expect judges and partners to care about reliability, latency, operator usability, safety, and path to deployment.
- Bring a simple fallback demo that works without perfect connectivity.
